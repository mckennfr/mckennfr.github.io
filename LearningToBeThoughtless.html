Summary

In this model, agents learn how to behave (what norm to adopt), using a strategy called Best Reply to Adaptive Sample Evidence, they also learn how much to think about how to behave.
  • How much agents think affects how they behave, which, given how others behave, affects how much they think.
  • Feedback between the social (inter-agent) and internal (intra-agent) dynamics is important.
  • The model generates stylized facts about the spatio-temporal evolution of norms: local conformity, global diversity, and punctuated equilibria.
